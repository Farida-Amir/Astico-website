About Us

WHO ARE WE?

AStiCO is an Egyptian company located in Cairo and started in 2013, we are specialized in exporting fresh and frozen agricultural products of prime quality.

WHAT WE DO?

AStiCO is one of the pioneers in the field of exporting fresh fruits and vegetables all over the world in Europe, Africa, and Asia, beside Arab countries. All year round, AStiCO provides a day-fresh range of fruits, and vegetables to its clients: importers, wholesalers, supermarkets, caterers and the processing industry. A diverse selection of high quality produce is available to pick from, guaranteed to satisfy. Our production includes grapes, strawberries, pomegranates, mangoes, red and white Garlic, gold and red onion, green and white beans, frozen broccoli, carrots, frozen artichoke, and many other fruits and vegetables of the finest quality. All our products are subject to international certifications auditing (Global GAP – Smeta – Fair Trade).
